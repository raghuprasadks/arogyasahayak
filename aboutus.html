Patanjali Yoga Samiti, Bangalore

 

 

Patanjali Yogpeeth (Trust), Haridwar is established by Param Pujya Swami Ramdev Ji to impart knowledge of yoga to all. It believes Yoga is a way of life and helps a person for overall personality development. Swami Ramdev has been conducting Yoga Camps in India and Abroad to spread the yoga. Patanjali Yoga Samiti, Bangalore has developed 'Patanjali Sahayak' for people in Bangalore to easily locate yoga centers.
